Central European Bank Announces Surprise Interest Rate Hike

Published: November 27, 2025

The Central European Bank (CEB) unexpectedly raised its benchmark interest rate by 50 basis points, citing persistent inflationary pressures across the bloc. The decision immediately impacted global stock markets, with indices falling across Asia and North America. Economists are debating whether this aggressive move will successfully stabilize consumer prices without triggering a sharp economic slowdown. The CEB President defended the action, calling it a necessary measure to ensure long-term stability and confidence in the currency.

Aid Pours In Following Major Earthquake in Southeast Asia

Published: November 26, 2025

International humanitarian aid and rescue teams have arrived in the quake-stricken region of Southeast Asia, following a 7.5 magnitude earthquake earlier this week. Governments and NGOs worldwide have pledged millions in immediate relief funding to provide temporary shelter, medical supplies, and food to the thousands displaced.
