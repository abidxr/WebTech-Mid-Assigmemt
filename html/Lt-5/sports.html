Published: November 27, 2025

Swimmer Sets New National Record at Olympic Qualifying Trials

Published: November 26, 2025

Twenty-year-old rising star, Alex Chen, broke the long-standing national 200m freestyle record during the final day of the Olympic qualifying trials. His time of 1:45.02 not only secured him a spot on the national team but also positions him as a strong medal contender for next summer's games. Chen credited his coach and intense high-altitude training for the breakthrough performance. The focus now shifts to international competition as he prepares for the biggest event of his career.
